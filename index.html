Calculate BMI

The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measurement that determines whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height. It is calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters. The resulting number is then compared to a range of BMI categories to assess if the person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. BMI is a widely used tool in health and fitness assessments.
